Ganga Ghat Cleanup & Community Development Initiative

Assi Ghat · 100th Plogathon

Public health, cleanliness, and sanitation are foundational to the well-being of Varanasi — one of India's most sacred cities. Recognising that the historical ghats and surrounding communities faced significant environmental health challenges, I initiated a series of community development projects aimed at raising awareness and driving measurable change.

In collaboration with the Priya Grih Udyog Vikas Avam Shiksha Prasar Samiti and Kudabazar, a dedicated Varanasi clean-up collective, the initiative enlisted upwards of 500 volunteers to clean the ghats. The effort resulted in the collection of 485 kilograms of recyclable waste — a tangible measure of its reach and impact.

I also led Kudabazar's awareness campaign through the 100th Plogathon at Assi Ghat, reinforcing our collective commitment under the motto — "Cleaner and Greener All the Way."
